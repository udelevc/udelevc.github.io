About Us



The goal of Formula SAE is to take the Engineering curriculum and apply it to a real-world industrial problem by building a prototype race car for a fictional single entry series. Student’s cars are not only judged based on their performance, but also the Engineering decisions the students have made in their design, weighing performance versus cost and reliability. Formula SAE challenges students to design a car that is quick, reliable, fuel efficient, and cost effective.


The Formula SAE Electric was started in 2015 as a senior design project to design and build the car. Since 2015, the Electric Vehicle Club has carried the torch to continue to develop and build the car. The team is mostly comprised of dedicated freshman and sophomore engineering students learning as we go.

Sponsorships



The University of Delaware Electric Vehicle Club relies on sponsors to fund the manufacturing of the car. With limited resources and the desire to make the car even better, we have to balance the manufacturing of the car and financial planning. We are excited for this year’s car, the support of our new sponsors, and continued support from past sponsors. This year’s team has much excitement and enthusiasm to make this year the best one yet.
